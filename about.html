a bit about me

my philosophy

I believe great things can happen when efficient engineering meets elegant design. I'm a coder and a dreamer, and I want to spend my career building products that help make life simple.

what i'm up to

I'm currently at IBM on the Interactive Experience team as a front-end experience developer. Staffed within IBM Community Health, I build and optimize the UX of applications that support closed-loop referral systems for a number of clients. Day to day, I design, pitch, and implement front-end user interfaces and workflows for these custom solutions.

Beyond all that, I spend my free time perfecting my baking skills (ask me about my raspberry jam bars) and painting greeting cards for friends and family. I love to golf when the Chicago weather allows, and recently picked up beach volleyball. But you cann often find me on a walk listening to TBOY or HIBT. Whether you'd like to talk about design, software development, or tiramisu, I'd love to hear from you!

education

I graduated from Northwestern University in 2021 with a major in Computer Science and two certificates in design and marketing. While on campus, I managed peers and developed custom apps as the CTO of Student Holdings, a non-profit student run business. I also volunteered web development and design services to local non profits through DISC.
